FAQ

Common questions about appliance repair in Silver Spring.

General Questions

Basic information before you schedule service.

What appliance repair services do you offer?
We provide washer repair, dryer repair, refrigerator repair, oven repair, stove repair, and dishwasher repair for homeowners across Greater Silver Spring.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Silver Spring and nearby neighborhoods throughout the Greater Silver Spring area. You can also view the service area page for more location details.
How can I schedule appliance repair?
You can call us at (240) 438-7799, email info@appliancerepairsilverspring.com, or use the contact page to send a service request.
What should I have ready before I call?
It helps to know the appliance type, the brand if available, the main issue you are seeing, and the address where service is needed.
Appliance Questions

Answers for common appliance problems.

Do you repair washers that will not spin or drain?
Yes. Washer issues such as failure to spin, failure to drain, unusual noise, or incomplete cycles are common reasons homeowners call for service.
Can you help if my dryer is not heating?
Yes. Dryers that stop heating, take too long to dry, or stop starting properly are all common service requests.
Do you work on refrigerators that are not cooling well?
We help with refrigerator issues such as poor cooling, leaks, unusual sounds, and ice maker concerns.
Can you repair ovens and stoves that stop heating properly?
Yes. We handle common oven and stove issues such as uneven heating, burner problems, ignition issues, and appliances that will not turn on.
Do you repair dishwashers that leak or do not drain?
Yes. Dishwasher problems such as leaking, poor drainage, or poor cleaning performance are all reasons to request service.
Scheduling And Service

Questions about booking and service visits.

What are your business hours?
Our business hours are 8 AM to 6 PM.
Is calling the fastest way to schedule service?
Yes. Calling is usually the fastest way to discuss the issue and coordinate a convenient service time.
Can I email my repair request instead?
Yes. You can email your request if that is more convenient, especially if you want to include written details about the issue.
Do I need to know the exact cause of the issue before contacting you?
No. A simple description of the main problem is enough. For example, you can tell us the appliance is leaking, not cooling, not heating, or not turning on.
Should I include my address when I request service?
Yes. Sharing the service address helps confirm that your home is within the area we cover and makes scheduling faster.
Repair Expectations

A few more answers homeowners often ask.

Can I contact you about more than one appliance problem?
Yes. If you are having trouble with more than one appliance, mention each issue when you call or send your request.
What if I am not sure whether the issue is serious?
You can still reach out. Even a short description of the symptoms helps explain what is happening so you can decide the next step more confidently.
Is it helpful to include the appliance brand or model?
Yes. If you know the brand or model, that information can be useful, but it is not required to contact us.
Can I use the contact page instead of calling?
Yes. The contact page is a good option if you prefer to send your information in writing instead of calling.
Where can I go if I still have questions?
If your question is not covered here, call us or send a message through the contact page and we will be happy to help.
